About us

We are a team of two profressional translators armed with many years of experience working as staff translators at various companies and institutions throughout Europe. Specialising in legal, financial and automotive texts, we enjoy a wide variety of subject matters and ensure our output always meets your high standards.

Having both worked as in-house translators, we know the ins and outs of the industry, and can show you exactly how to get the best value for money.

We are confident users of the CAT tools SDL Studio, Wordfast, MemoQ, XTM, WordBee and Across and would be keen to get to know any other translation tools that you work with.

On a personal level, we have been very close friends for eight years and have built an excellent rapport that has translated very well into a profressional relationhip. As of recently, we live a five-minute walk away from one another, meaning we predominately work in eachother's home offices, sharing ideas and experience day-in, day-out.
